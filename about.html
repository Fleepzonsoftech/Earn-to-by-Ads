About Earn to by Ads

Earn to by Ads is an engaging Android application designed to reward you for interacting with advertisements and participating in daily activities. Our goal is to provide a fun and easy way for you to accumulate virtual coins that can potentially be redeemed for exciting rewards in the future.

Key Features

  • Seamless Onboarding: Splash screen and automatic transition to login/signup.
  • Home Page Dashboard: Menu Drawer with your wallet, coupons, referral code, and more.
  • Earn by Watching Ads: Watch 10 rewarded video ads to earn coins.
  • Referral Program: Share your code and earn coins when someone signs up using it.
  • Daily Login Bonus: Earn coins for each day's first login.
  • Spin Wheel: 5 daily spins to win coins or coupons.
  • No Internet Handling: Offline popup with retry option.
  • Authentication & Features: Secure signup/login with referral support and password reset via PIN. Other features are available in the app; check by installing it from Google Play Store and exploring the menu.

Ad Disclaimer

Users are never required to click on ads to earn rewards. Rewards are only granted for viewing ads as intended by the advertiser.

Age Restriction
