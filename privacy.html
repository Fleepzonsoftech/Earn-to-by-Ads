1. Introduction

Welcome to Earn to by Ads. By using this app, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe user experience.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly beyond what is necessary for app functionality. Third-party partners (Google AdMob, Firebase, etc.) may automatically collect certain data.

Information collected during app usage includes:

  • Signup: Username, Email address, Password (stored securely in Firebase), Optional referral codes, 4-digit PIN (for account verification and password recovery)
  • Login: Email and password for authentication, Session data for app access
  • Forgot Password: Email address to request a password reset; Users receive a password reset link via email; 4-digit PIN may be used for verification during reset
  • Other automatic collection by third-party services: Device information (model, OS, unique identifiers), Advertising ID (for personalized/non-personalized ads), Usage data (ad views, app engagement, crash reports)

3. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts (Signup, Login, Forgot Password)
  • Deliver relevant ads via trusted ad networks
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide in-app rewards (coins, spins, or coupons) only for watching ads

4. Rewards & Ads

Users earn coins, spins, or coupons only by watching ads. No rewards are given for clicking ads, in compliance with Google AdMob policies.

10. Data Deletion / User Rights

Users have the right to delete their profile and data permanently. To delete your profile:

  1. Open the Earn to by Ads app.
  2. Tap the Menu (three-line icon) in the top navigation bar.
  3. Select Delete Profile from the menu.
  4. Enter your account details for verification.
  5. Tap the Delete button.

Your profile and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers and Firebase.
