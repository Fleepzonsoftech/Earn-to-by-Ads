Earn to by Ads — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October-28-2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Earn to by Ads mobile application.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Earn to by Ads. By using this app, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, compliant user experience in accordance with Google Platforms Program Policies.

2. Ads and Rewards

Ads in this app are served exclusively by Google AdMob. Users are never required to click on ads to earn rewards; rewards are granted only for completing app tasks. Rewarded ads, where available, are opt-in and clearly labeled before viewing.

3. Personalized Ads and Consent

We may request your consent to display personalized ads in compliance with GDPR and CCPA. Users can opt out at any time via their device’s ad settings or Google Ad Settings. Ads are not targeted based on sensitive information, including health, race, sexual orientation, political beliefs, financial status, or activity of children under 13.

4. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us at helpsearntoads@gmail.com.

5. Information We Collect

  • Signup/Login: Username, email, password, optional referral codes, 4-digit PIN.
  • Automatic Collection: Device information, Advertising ID, ad views, app engagement, crash reports.
  • Optional Features: Profile photo uploads (with explicit user consent).

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Provides ads and collects advertising and usage data. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase: Collects crash reports, authentication, and analytics data. See Firebase Privacy & Security.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

8. Data Deletion & User Rights

Users can permanently delete their profile and all associated data directly from the app using the "Delete Profile" option in account settings. Requests can also be sent to helpsearntoads@gmail.com.

9. Location Data

We do not collect precise location data (GPS, Wi-Fi, or cell tower) without explicit opt-in consent. Any collected location data is transmitted securely.

10. Compliance with Google Policies

  • Ads are not targeted to children under 13.
  • No collection or use of sensitive information for ad targeting.
  • Users are never required to click ads to earn rewards.
  • Consent is obtained for personalized ads, with opt-out options provided.
  • All user data is protected and not shared for illegal or prohibited activities.
  • Rewarded ads are opt-in only and clearly described.
  • Users can delete all personal data using the in-app option.

11. Policy Updates

Privacy Policy updates will be posted here with the revised date. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.

12. Contact

For questions or concerns, contact us at: helpsearntoads@gmail.com
